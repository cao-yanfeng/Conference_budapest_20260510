2026 International Forum on

Budapest | October 12–14, 2026

About the Conference

This is an international forum in the field of fire safety science research and fire protection development, aims at promoting international cooperation in fire safety science and the application of new fire protection technologies. The forum is jointly organized by four universities, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (China), University of Miskolc (Hungary), Ludovica National University of Public Service (Hungary), and Technical University in Zvolen (Slovakia). The forum will be held on the campus of Ludovica National University of Public Service in Budapest, the capital of Hungary, October 12 to 14, 2026.

Conference Topics

Fire Safety Science Research

  • Characterization of Material Flammability
  • Fire Spread
  • Research on Flame-retardant Materials

Advanced Fire Protection Technologies & Applications

  • Drone-based Early Warning and Firefighting
  • Development of Environmentally Friendly Fire Extinguishing Agents
  • Non-destructive Fire Protection for Historic Buildings
